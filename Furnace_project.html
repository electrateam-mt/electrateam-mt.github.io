Intelligent Processing:

Powered by the STM32F4 processor, the controller efficiently manages tasks. It measures temperatures and monitors the environment simultaneously, updating the display accordingly. This ensures quick responses to any issues and reliable relay activation.

Redundant Temperature Sensors:

To ensure reliable temperature monitoring, multiple sensors are utilized. Even if one sensor malfunctions, the controller maintains accurate readings and alerts you if any sensor requires attention.

Safety First:

The Smart Furnace Controller prioritizes safety. A gyroscope sensor detects tilting, minimizing potential accidents. Advanced gas sensors monitor for CO2, CO, and smoke, providing timely alarms during emergencies.

Efficient Operation:

The Smart Furnace Controller simplifies furnace operation. It uses an ignition device to start the flame, and temperature sensors maintain the desired heat level. When the set temperature is reached, the flame turns off, conserving energy. Once the temperature drops, it reignites automatically, providing continuous warmth.

Flame Ionization Detection:

Our precise flame detection system utilizes the Flame Ionization Detection method. By converting weak flame currents into readable voltage, the controller quickly responds to flame changes.
